Supportive software download

  • Russian recoder (Russian: Alt/KOI-8/KOI-7/Main/Windows/ISO, NewLine: MS-DOS/UNIX) and Russian transliteration recoder (rather old version) that recodes <Transliterated -> Alternative> Example of such transliteration: "Odnazhdy v studenuju zimnjuyu poru".
    • MS-DOS executable and C sources in ARJ archive and ZIP archive.
    • *NIX C sources in TGZ archive.
    • Latest version with PERL scripts.
    • ReCoder written in JavaScript, 2002, recodes online in your web browser.
  • MS-DOS screen and keyboard drivers & screen remappers or screen remappers only.
